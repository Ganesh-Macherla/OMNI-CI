## Placements at  SNU Chennai
Placements at Shiv Nadar University Chennai is being handled by the same team which has churned out excellent placements for SSN over the years. In addition to encouraging students to place in top companies, the career development centre is also instrumental in preparing students for various career opportunities and higher education through focused training, industry exposure, and lectures by top practitioners from various fields and internships.
#### Placements Team
The SNU Chennai placement team is dedicated to helping students secure their dream jobs. The team works closely with students to develop their resumes, prepare for interviews, and network with potential employers. The team also organizes a variety of events and activities to help students gain exposure to the corporate world, such as industry talks, hackathons, and internship opportunities.
#### Placement Training Program
The SNU Chennai placement training program is designed to help students develop the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in the job market. The program covers a variety of topics, including aptitude testing, technical skills, soft skills, and resume writing. The program also includes mock interviews and networking opportunities.
#### Industry Exposure
SNU Chennai offers a variety of opportunities for students to gain industry exposure. The university partners with leading companies to offer internships and co-op placements. Students can also participate in hackathons and other competitions to showcase their skills.
