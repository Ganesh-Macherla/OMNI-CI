## Research at  SNU Chennai
Shiv Nadar University Chennai places special emphasis on cutting edge research and encourages its faculty and students to involve in research-oriented activities. It provides state-of-the-art infrastructure and access to existing research. Apart from this, it promotes collaboration with funding agencies and other institutions and industry for research. SIRS is a unique programme that instils a research aptitude and nurtures a research orientation among the undergraduate students even in their first year at the university.
## Heuresis The Research Day
This event will be a dedicated platform to recognize and showcase the outstanding research, innovative projects, and academic excellence demonstrated by our university community. Research Day is an opportunity for scholars, researchers, and students from all disciplines to come together to share their work, exchange ideas, and foster a collaborative spirit that fuels innovation and discovery. It is also a time to celebrate the commitment and dedication that drive our community toward academic and scientific advancement.
