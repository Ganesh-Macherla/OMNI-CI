The Shiv Nadar School of Law is Shiv Nadar University (SNU) Chennai’s newest academic program. It is situated on the SNU Chennai campus, which boasts well-equipped libraries, world-class research facilities, and 230 acres of greenery. SNU Chennai is a hub for intellectual exploration and innovation, and the Shiv Nadar School of Law stands poised to cultivate India’s best legal professionals and scholars.
