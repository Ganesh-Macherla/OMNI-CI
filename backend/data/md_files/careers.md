## Faculty - Minimum Qualification & Experience 
[ Join Us ](https://www.snuchennai.edu.in/faculty-application-form/)
#### Professor
Ph.D. in the relevant field with 12 years of experience out of which at least three (3) years at the level of Associate Professor; Minimum ten (10) publications in the peer reviewed SCI journal, of which at least two (2) publications in the last three (3) years; Completion of supervision of at least two (2) doctoral research scholars; Should have been awarded a project or consulting work, worth of Rs. 20 Lakhs.
#### Associate Professor
Ph.D. in the relevant field with a minimum of eight (8) years of post-Ph.D. experience at the level of Assistant Professor; Minimum eight (8) publications in the peer reviewed SCI journal, of which at least two (2) publications in the last three (3) years; Completion of supervision of at least one (1) doctoral research scholar; should have been awarded a project or consulting work, worth of Rs. 10 Lakhs.
#### Assistant Professor   
Grade III
Ph.D. in the relevant field with at least five (5) years of post-Ph.D. experience. Four (4) publications in the peer reviewed SCI Journals out of which at least two (2) publications in the last three (3) years. and One (1) in a reputed conference
#### Assistant Professor   
Grade II
Ph.D. in the relevant field with at least two (2) or more years of post-Ph.D. experience. Three (3) publications in the peer reviewed SCI Journals and One (1) in a reputed conference.
#### Assistant Professor   
Grade I
Ph.D. in the relevant field with less than 2 years of post-Ph.D. experience. Two (2) publications in the peer reviewed SCI Journals and One (1) in a reputed conference. For Law, NET qualified candidates with master’s degree will also be considered
Note: The above prescriptions are indicative, and the selection committee is empowered to make exceptions for deserving candidates.Salary: Commensurate with qualification and experience as per 7th Pay Commission with DA as per the state government (currently DA is 38% of Basic Pay)  
Additional Benefits:
  * Faculty Professional Development Fund – Rs. 3 Lakhs over a period of 3 years
  * Life and Medical Insurance Coverage
  * Social Security – PF and Gratuity
  * Faculty Research Incentive (FRI) Program; Seed Money for an approved research work; Incentive for patents and publication of research papers in reputed journals.
  * Faculty Performance Incentive Program (FPIP)
  * Health Checkup, Maternity and other leaves
